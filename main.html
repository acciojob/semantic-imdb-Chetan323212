Summary:Batman faces his greatest threat yet when the Joker, a chaotic criminal mastermind, unleashes a reign of terror on Gotham City. The Joker's goal isn't money or power — he wants to prove that anyone can be driven to madness and evil, given the right push. Meanwhile, idealistic district attorney Harvey Dent rises as Gotham's "White Knight," the city's hope for justice. As Batman and Dent team up to take down the mob, the Joker systematically dismantles everything Bruce Wayne holds dear — forcing Batman to make impossible moral choices. Ultimately, Harvey Dent is broken by tragedy and transforms into the villain Two-Face, leaving Batman to take the blame for his crimes — sacrificing his own reputation to preserve Gotham's hope.
