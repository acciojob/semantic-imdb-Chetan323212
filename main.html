Synopsis

When the Joker — a sadistic agent of chaos — emerges from Gotham's criminal underworld, Batman must confront the darkest impulses of the city he swore to protect. With the fate of Gotham's soul hanging on the choices of its heroes, the line between justice and vigilantism begins to dissolve.

Famous Quote

"Why so serious?"
— The Joker, portrayed by Heath Ledger

Perhaps the most iconic line in modern cinema, the Joker's taunting question encapsulates his entire philosophy — that life's tragedies are better met with a smile, and that the veneer of civilisation is thinner than anyone dares to admit.
